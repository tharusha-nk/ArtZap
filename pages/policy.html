Privacy Policy

Welcome to ArtZap. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting the personal information you provide. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your data when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you make a purchase, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact us, we may collect details such as your name, email, phone number, and shipping address.
  • Payment Information: We process payments through secure third-party services and do not store your credit/debit card details.
  • Browsing Data: We collect non-personal data such as IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited to enhance user experience.
  • Cookies: Our website may use cookies to remember your preferences and improve functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your orders efficiently.
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • To send updates, promotions, and newsletters (you can opt out anytime).
  • To enhance website security and prevent fraud.
  • To analyze website performance and improve our services.

3. How We Protect Your Information

  • We implement security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, theft, or misuse.
  • Personal information is stored securely and only accessed by authorized personnel.
  • We use encryption and secure payment gateways for financial transactions.

4. Sharing of Information

  • We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.
  • We may share information with trusted service providers for payment processing, shipping, and customer support.
  • If required by law, we may disclose information to legal authorities.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

  • Cookies help us enhance your browsing experience and provide personalized content.
  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not function properly.
  • We use analytics tools to track website usage and improve performance.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • You can opt out of marketing emails by clicking the "unsubscribe" link.
  • If you have privacy concerns, contact us for assistance.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically.
